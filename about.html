ABOUT

TRAINING & CREDENTIALS

I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) in New York State.

I received my Master of Social Work from Hunter College School of Social Work in New York City.

I am trained in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) therapy and continue to receive ongoing training in psychodynamic and experiential therapeutic approaches.

INSURANCE & FEES

I am an out-of-network provider. This means I do not bill insurance directly, but I can provide you with a superbill (detailed receipt) that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement.

Many insurance plans offer out-of-network benefits. I recommend contacting your insurance provider to understand your coverage.

Please reach out to discuss fees and payment options during our consultation.
